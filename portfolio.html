Skincare & Beauty · Design + Development

Lulu's Dose of Beauty

A multi-channel skincare and creative brand needed a professional home base to match its growing presence on TikTok, in retail, and through digital art commissions — with no existing site to build from.

// The Challenge

No central site to unify the brand's skincare consulting, retail, and creative offerings, or to convert social followers into customers.

// What We Did

Built the site from scratch — custom brand identity (a dark plum and warm gold "kintsugi" palette), a responsive layout, image galleries for product and art commissions, and deployment via GitHub Pages.

// The Result

A live, fully functioning site driving traffic from social — with unsolicited positive feedback from followers on the design.
