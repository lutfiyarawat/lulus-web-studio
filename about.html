// Our Story

Built by someone who's run a business before a client ever hired her to design one.

LULUS Web Studio was founded on a simple idea: a website should work like a member of your sales team, not just sit there as a digital business card.

Before designing sites for other businesses, our founder built and ran her own — learning direct sales, client relationships, and what actually gets a customer to say yes, long before learning to code. That background shapes every project: design decisions are made for conversion first, aesthetics second — because the two should never compete.

Every site is planned, designed, and built with the same care as a business owner building their own brand from the ground up. Because that's exactly the experience behind it.
